Eggs

Description:

Scrambled eggs are another classic dish. Mainly eaten for breakfast but can be made for a quick protein boost. A great dish which can have as much flavor and optional ingredients as you like.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Heat a pan to medium heat
  2. Crack Eggs into a bowl
  3. Add some milk
  4. Mix eggs and milk until all yokes are broken and mixed
  5. Cook in the buttered pan
  6. Enjoy!
