Hot Dogs

Description:

An American classic dish. Hot dogs have been around since the German immigrants brought them over in the 1860s.

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Dogs
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • Any other topping you like

Directions:

  1. Heat Grill
  2. Cook dogs until you et nice grill marks
  3. Heat buns (Optional)
  4. Enjoy!
