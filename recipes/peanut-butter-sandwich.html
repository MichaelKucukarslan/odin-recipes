Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description:

The classic quick peanut butter sandwich is both fast to make and filling. Starting out as a snack for upper class "elegant lady" luncheons in the early 19th century the peanut butter sandwich is now a classic after school snack. Depending on what else is added to the dish it can also be very healthy.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Optional)
  • Bananas (Optional)

Directions:

  1. Get your favorite bread
  2. Toast it (Optional)
  3. Spread your favorite Peanut Butter on it
  4. Enjoy!
