Odin Recipe Project

A short page of 4 simple recipes. These are simple meals just to show how a simple static website is made. It will not have a lot of the bells and whistles that most users are used to seeing. Things like a floating header that is the same on every page or pictures of the dishes. There are pictures in each recipe of what your dish could look like. Enjoy!
